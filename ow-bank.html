Skins


There are 885 skins in total.


  • 68 are Default Skins.


  • 148 are Rare Skins unlocked by buying them with Coins / Legacy Credits.


  • 190 are Epic Skins not from the OWL unlocked by Coins / Legacy Credits, either Battle Pass, Shop or Events.


  • 474 are Legendary Skins unlocked by Coins / Legacy Credits, either Battle Pass, Shop or Events.


  • 5 are Mythic Skins unlocked by getting to Tier 80 in Season 1-5 Battle Pass.


  • 25 are Overwatch League Skins that arent team based and are unlocked by buying their respective bundles.

  • 6 were unlocked by buying the Blizzcon Virtual Ticket.

  • 13 were unlocked by participating in OW1 Skin-Based events.

  • 48 were unlocked by participating in the "Weekly Challenges" of OW1 Events.

  • 1 was available during a charity event funding Breast Cancer Research.
